What is weeble about?

Weeble is an all exclusive platform that gives young people an opportunity to better themselves and their skills in life.
From its founder, weeble has caught the attention of many even before it was launched. It allows the users to interact both socially and educationaly by creating an atmosphere that is condusive for both.
Students are allowed to do their assignments both individually and in groups while weeble provides a box to the top right where chats can be seen and a video button at the top left where group calls can be made instantly.


How much is it to access weeble?

Weeble is a site that promotes education for all and is therefore free for all. All that is needed is an internet connection.


Why weeble?

Weeble is an exclusive sight that is free and easy to use. It is also easily accessible and requires no requirement specifications.
